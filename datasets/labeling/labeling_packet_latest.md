# Student Labeling Packet

Use this packet to quickly rate whether generated parts are usable.

## Quick Rubric
1. `playable_yes_no`: `yes` if a middle-schooler can read and play it after light practice.
2. `resembles_song_1_to_5`: 1 = not recognizable, 5 = clearly sounds like the song.
3. `difficulty_1_to_5`: 1 = very easy, 5 = too hard for class.
4. `confidence_1_to_5`: 1 = guessing, 5 = very sure.

Keep each rating under 30 seconds. Short notes in `notes` are enough.
